The History of M&M is divided into numerous eras, not all nations or cultures have the same name or definition for select eras as such we will go off the more popular names for them in lore, with any special names being written as an footnote later. The Eras of History as we will call them are ordered as such:

- [[2- Magic & Mayhem - History/The Era Of The Gods\|The Era Of The Gods]]
- The Era Of The Ancients
- The Era Of The Emperors
- The Era Of The Kings
- The Era of The Struggle

The earliest date in lore is 1500BR and the latest is 1982PR, to put things into perspective that is close to 3500 years of lore, 3482 years to be exact, now my goal is to make it as detailed possible and that will take time, as such this wiki will be updated until the day where all the history of M&M can be considered complete. That aside, if you aren't really interested in the history of the world but just a nation or two, please take a look at Nations or if its Continents you want. Each will have their respective pages of history.
