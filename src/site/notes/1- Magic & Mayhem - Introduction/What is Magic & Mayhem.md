Magic & Mayhem or M&M as we will continue to call it for the ease of mouth, is the brain child of a one month writing marathon done by yours truly Yanji and the help/feedback of many others (Vinny, Parven, Silver, Zug etc..). The initial draft had numerous spelling mistakes, lore expositions and unnecessary focus points that hopefully this wiki can fix or make better and maybe even expand upon.

M&M is supposed to be a DnD world built on politics and a dose of well mayhem. You will see depictions of truly inhumane and disgusting actions alongside ones that will make you laugh. Needless to say, any action done or supported by characters in this universe is not what i believe in or think the world should be, i just wanted to make a world i would love to roleplay in, and i do love depressing stuff, so this tracks.

For now, this wiki is mostly focused on the lore and its goal is to organize it so that any future players of it can have somewhere to start. Now, the lore itself is divided into sections:

- [[2- Magic & Mayhem - History/History\|History]]
- Faiths
- Races
- Magic
- Map

Now of course, there is divisions inside of these sections but that's for you to find out. For now, go on and explore M&M, i hope you like it.
