*The Continent of Tholoth, originally named Zeovrulen, is a continent of many biomes and fantastical creatures with the essence of magic and fantasy being far more common in Tholoth compared to the other continents. It primarily houses the Tholothii with no other species native to the region or if they were, they have been lost to history. Tholoth has a diverse range of geological features from mountain ranges to flat steppes to full rain forests, its people, the Tholothii, have divided into numerous cultures based on such features with some being closely defined by their home's appearance and way of life.*

*Politically, Tholoth is officially united under the Eternal Nelaeryn Empire, Nelaeryn Empire for short, and even though to outsiders it looks to be a truly unified continent, internally the empire in 1982PR is divided in its administrative authority with regions directly under the Nelaeryn clan, aside from North Nelaeryn, directly answering to the regency council while entire provinces are either administered by their respective major clan or a council of smaller clans. Tholoth is also officially closed continent but it is not hard to find merchants from the other continents in east Tholoth as the east suffers from a lack of direct imperial control.*
